Introduction

This web-app makes organizing home tasks possible. This web-page is divided into different apps:

  • Tasks
  • Groceries
  • Finances
  • Meal-planning
Each of those apps organizes different areas. In the following cards, each of those areas and the possible functions of those tasks are explained further.

Tasks/Calendar

The task app offers multiple functions:

  • Scheudle Tasks
  • Recurring Tasks
  • Planned Events
It is possible to scheudle future tasks and automate task scheudleing for recurring tasks. Furthermore planned events can be registered beforehand.

Groceries

The groceries app helps organize grocery shopping. Furthermore bought items are tracked and sorted after categories so that the data can be analysed and visualized later on.

Finance

The finance app helps keep track of expenditures. It lists the cost of everything in the household. Furthermore, it displays past months therby providing points of comparison. Expenditures are divided into different categories, like groceries, clothing, entertainment, subscriptions and freetime-activities.

Meal-planning

The meal planning app helps to organize and plan meals. You can choose from different recipes and add needed ingredients to the grocery-list. All cooked meals are tracked and the data of what has been cooked can be displayed in form of different diagrams.
